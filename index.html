Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is PagePal really free?

Yes! Translation and summarization are completely unlimited and free forever. Advanced features (chat, YouTube, phishing) have generous daily quotas: 5 chats, 3 YouTube summaries, and 3 phishing checks per day. Quotas reset at midnight.

🔒 Do you store my data or browsing history?

No! PagePal is 100% privacy-focused. Your API keys are stored locally on your device only. Page content is sent directly to your chosen AI provider (Google, OpenAI, etc.), never to our servers. We don't collect, store, or track any of your data.

🤖 Which AI provider should I choose?

Chrome Local AI: Best for privacy (100% offline, no API key needed). Google Gemini: Best overall (free, fast, 1,500 requests/day). ChatGPT: Best quality (affordable, ~$0.15 per 1M tokens). Perplexity: Advanced AI (requires paid plan).

💰 What happens if I hit the daily free quota?

Your quotas automatically reset at midnight every day. You'll see a countdown timer showing exactly when they reset. Translation and summarization remain unlimited even if you hit other quotas. Upgrade to PRO (₪15/month or ₪99/year) for unlimited everything.

📄 Does it work on PDFs?

Yes! PDF support (translate, summarize, chat) is available for PRO users (₪15/month or ₪99/year). Works with both online PDFs and local files. Perfect for research papers, contracts, and business documents.

🎬 Does it work on YouTube?

Yes! PagePal extracts video captions/transcripts and provides summaries with timestamps (e.g., "At 05:30 the speaker explains..."). Videos must have captions (auto-generated works too). Free tier: 3 summaries per day. PRO: unlimited.

🌍 What languages are supported?

PagePal supports translation to/from 12 languages: English, Hebrew, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Arabic. The UI is primarily in Hebrew and English.

🎯 What are "Smart Prompts" and how do they work?

Smart Prompts are pre-built, domain-specific questions that automatically adapt to your content. Instead of thinking "what should I ask?", just select your field (Cybersecurity, Real Estate, Legal, Academic, Business, Code, Medical, News) and click. PagePal asks expert-level questions optimized for that domain. For example: on a security blog → "Extract CVE numbers and severity", on a property listing → "Calculate ROI based on price", on a contract → "What are my termination rights?". It's like having a domain expert helping you read!

💳 How do I upgrade to PRO?

Click "Upgrade to PRO" in the extension → Complete payment via Lemon Squeezy (secure payment processor) → Receive license key via email → Enter key in PagePal settings → Enjoy unlimited features! 14-day money-back guarantee.

🛡️ Is PagePal secure?

Yes! PagePal has an A- security rating (90/100) after comprehensive security audits. Features: XSS protection, minimal Chrome permissions, local-only API key storage, browser fingerprinting for quota enforcement, and zero data collection.

💻 Can I use it offline?

Yes, if you use Chrome Local AI (Gemini Nano). It's 100% offline and private, no internet required. Cloud AI providers (Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity) require an internet connection.
